Three Project Ideas

The Art of Watchmaking

A website about the history and mechanics of watchmaking, showing how the small, intricate parts of a watch work together.

The Journey of Coffee: From Bean to Cup

A site that explains the step-by-step process of how coffee gets from the plant to your cup, covering harvesting, roasting, and brewing.

The Golden Age of Space Exploration

A page telling the story of the Apollo space program and the missions to the Moon, using historical photos and a simple timeline.
